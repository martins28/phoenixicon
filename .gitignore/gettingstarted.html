Getting Started

  • Account Registration and Familiarity with the Account Panel

    To begin the registration process, click "Register" on the navigation bar on the Home page. Fill out all the fields in the form presented then click "signup".

    Your registration process is now complete. Enter your username and password to log into your personal account. In your account, you can see your investment package, account balance and the deposit and withdrawal tabs etc.

  • Investment

    PhoenixIcon uses some popular payment solutions. These include;

    • Perfect Money
    • Western Union
    • MoneyGram
    • Bitcoin

    PhoenixIcon invests clients funds on some of the most reliable and reputable platforms including 24options and Banc de Binary. The minimum investment amount is $5000 and the minimum investment period is 30 days.

    Click the deposit tab on your account and follow the instructions given. Your account balance will be topped up with the appropriate amount within 24 hours. In case of difficulties, contact our customer service via email on contact@phoenixicon.com and we will get back to you via the email address registered to your account within ten minutes.

  • Withdrawal of Funds

    You can withdraw profits by clicking the withdrawal tab in the navigation bar after you have successfully logged into your account, follow the instructions and correctly fill in the form provided with your preffered method of withdrawal. You may also want to allow your profits to accumalate and withdraw once monthly.

    Before withdrawal, please ensure that your e-wallet or account details are correctly entered to avoid errors.

    PhoenixIcon sends payments using several platforms such as:

    • Perfect Money
    • Bitcoin
    • Western Union
    • MoneyGram

  • Meaning of Trading Terms
    • Limited risk: This is the original investment on your account.
    • Profit/Loss: This is the difference between the profit and the losses made on your account.
    • Equity: This is limited risk + profit/loss.
    • Bonus: The bonus amount in your account.
    • Available to deal: The total money available that can be traded on.
