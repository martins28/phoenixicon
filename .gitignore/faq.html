Frequently asked questions

How do I register

To start the registration process, you click "register" under start button on the home page, fill out all the lines in the window that appears, enter your personal details, then submit; Registration is complete and you can log in your personal account. In your account you can see your investment packages, account statement and summary, deposit and withdraw tabs, etc.

Why we accept Western Union and Moneygram

We accept western union, moneygram because of the high tax rate attached to usage of other money transfer services and the fact that these ones we use are easier to monitor.

What is the minimum investment amount?

The minimum amount for investment is $5000. Minimum investment period of 1 day. To deposit, click the "deposit" tab in your account and choose the investment package, enter the amount, select your payment details and confirm your deposit. The changes will apear in your personal account within 24hours and trades commences immediately.

Referral system

The project provides additional income on your account via link referrals. The percentage referral program pays 3% of the amount of the Deposit made by your referral to the investor. The Commission is credited to your balance automatically. If you want to earn more, you need to attract more investors to your link. You can use any type of advertising for our project on different resources on the Internet, social networks etc.

Withdrawal of funds

After 24hours of investment, in the personal account you will receive the accrued interest. You can withdraw the entire Deposit and the amount of profit, or only the amount of profit. You can also make the investment of Deposit or add funds to your account or simply do nothing and allow the account grow. Before withdrawal, make sure that your e-wallet address or account is properly enered to avoid errors. Phoenixicon sends payout through various e-currencies like perfect money, Bitcoin, Payza, Western Union and Moneygram.

Meaning of Trade terms

  • Limited risk: This is the original investment on your account.
  • Profit/Loss: This is the difference between the profit and the losses made on your account.
  • Equity: This is limited risk + profit/loss.
  • Bonus: The bonus amount in your account.
  • Available to deal: The total money available that can be traded on.
